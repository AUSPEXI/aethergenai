Ablation Testing Explained: What Actually Moves the Needle

Why Ablate

  • Distinguish signal from noise
  • Guide roadmap toward high‑leverage components
  • Communicate value with clear deltas

Patterns

  • One‑at‑a‑time feature drops
  • Module on/off toggles
  • Interaction checks and stress sweeps

Reporting

We publish deltas with confidence intervals and include the ablation trace in the evidence bundle. Reviewers can audit what changed performance and by how much.
