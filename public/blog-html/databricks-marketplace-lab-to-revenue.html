Executive Summary

AethergenPlatform turns experimental datasets and niche models into Databricks Marketplace listings with procurement‑grade evidence. You get packaging, validation, and automated listing artifacts—so buyers can evaluate, adopt, and pay without months of red tape.

Who This Is For

  • Data teams with high‑value synthetic datasets or harmonised corpora.
  • Model builders offering specialized detectors or LLM adapters for regulated domains.
  • Enterprises needing private listings and entitlements mapped to legal/compliance.

Buyer Expectations (What Converts)

  • Evidence: clear, reproducible metrics at declared operating points.
  • Documentation: schema, intended use, limits, and support policy.
  • Packaging: install scripts, Unity Catalog entries, and samples.
  • Pricing clarity: tiers, entitlements, and compute ownership.
  • Trial flows: sandboxable assets that prove value fast.

Packaging: Datasets

  • Format: Delta/Parquet with Unity Catalog registration.
  • Schema docs: field types, ranges, constraints, vocabularies.
  • Samples: small public slice for quick exploration.
  • Evidence: fidelity/utility metrics, drift baselines, CIs.
  • Licensing: usage scope, redistribution limits, retention.

Packaging: Models

  • Formats: MLflow, ONNX, or GGUF (edge) with device notes.
  • Inference notebooks: examples at target operating points.
  • Calibration: threshold selection guidance and SOPs.
  • Evidence: utility, segment stability, robustness tests.
  • Limits: intended use, known failure modes, guardrails.

Evidence Bundle (Marketplace‑Ready)

  • Metrics with confidence intervals; plots for buyers and auditors.
  • Ablation tables showing what truly drives performance.
  • Privacy notes (if synthetic) and optional DP budgets.
  • Seeds/config/hash manifest for reproducibility.
  • Support policy, SLAs, upgrade cadence.

Listing Artifacts

  • README with value proposition, evidence highlights, and CTA.
  • Unity Catalog object definitions (schemas, grants).
  • Install notebooks and sample queries.
  • Pricing & entitlements JSON for self‑serve checkout.
  • Change‑log and migration notes.

Promotion Gates

  • Evidence thresholds pass at chosen operating points.
  • Stability across declared segments within tolerance.
  • Packaging validation (schema, notebooks, tests) is green.
  • Legal review: license terms, export controls, usage limits.
  • Support readiness: runbook, escalation, on‑call rotation.

Unity Catalog & Governance

  • Secure sharing to target workspaces with fine‑grained grants.
  • Audit trails for listing access and asset downloads.
  • Lineage from raw seeds to packaged assets.
  • Automated schema diffs and deprecation notices.

Pricing & Entitlements

  • Self‑Service: fixed monthly/annual; basic support; asset updates included.
  • Assisted: premium updates, limited custom fields, guided setup.
  • Full‑Service: private listings, SLAs, customization, and shared roadmap.
  • Compute ownership: buyer pays for inference/ETL; we price by rights and refresh.

Trial Design
