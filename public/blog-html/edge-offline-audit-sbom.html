Offline Edge Audit: SBOM, Checksums, and QR‑Verified Manifests

Air‑gapped deployments still need strong audit trails. AethergenPlatform packages offline edge artifacts with SBOMs, signed manifests, and QR‑encoded hashes so field engineers and auditors can verify what’s running—without a network.

Why This Matters

Factories, fleets, and defence environments often operate partially or fully offline. Trust depends on reproducible builds, verifiable artifacts, and on‑site checks.

How We Package
