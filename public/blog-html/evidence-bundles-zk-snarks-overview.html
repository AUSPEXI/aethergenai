Evidence Bundles and zk‑Proofs: Verifying Claims Without IP Leakage

Buyers need to verify privacy bounds and performance claims without access to proprietary code. AethergenPlatform ships evidence bundles—signed JSON with metrics, lineage, and ablations—and can add zero‑knowledge proofs to attest privacy constraints without leaking internals.

What’s Inside an Evidence Bundle

  • Lineage and recipe hashes
  • Utility and privacy metrics
  • Ablation traces and effect sizes
  • Artifact checksums and signatures

Optional zk‑Proofs

For sensitive deployments, we attach zk‑proofs that demonstrate bounds (e.g., DP ε thresholds or aggregate constraints) while keeping raw data and parameters hidden.
