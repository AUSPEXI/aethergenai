From First Pilot to Policy: Building Trust with Quality Gates

By Gwylym Owen — 20–30 min read

Executive Summary

Pilots stall when results aren’t reproducible or reviewable. AethergenPlatform turns pilots into policy by shipping quality gates and evidence bundles: OP utility with CIs, stability bands, latency SLOs, and privacy probes—signed and ready for audit.

Define the Gate

  • Operating point (OP) tied to analyst capacity or safety budget.
  • Stability bands across segments (region/product/lifecycle).
  • Latency p95/p99 envelopes; privacy probe thresholds.

Pilot SOP

  1. Freeze OP and segment taxonomy with stakeholders.
  2. Run evaluation; compute CIs; generate dashboards.
  3. Package evidence bundle (metrics, plots, configs, sbom, manifest).
  4. Review in change‑control; rehearse rollback.

Policy Promotion

  • Fail‑closed gates in CI guard promotion.
  • Catalog comments reference evidence IDs and OP thresholds.
  • SLAs reference OP/stability bands and refresh cadence.
