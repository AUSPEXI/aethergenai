From Starlings to Swarms: 8D Safety for Thousands of Drones

This article outlines how AethergenPlatform’s 8D manifold control and non‑linear methods enable large‑scale, resilient swarms (1k–15k+) with evidence‑backed safety—sharing the safety/evaluation concepts without disclosing proprietary control laws.

8D State Manifold

An agent’s control state lives on an 8‑dimensional manifold capturing kinematics and mission/safety context: position/velocity plus health/risk and link/latency/role. Variants include local density, goal progress, altitude band, or airspace mode.

Topological Flocking

Each drone tracks ≈7 nearest neighbors by graph connectivity, improving resilience in variable density (starling heuristic). Fields and SDFs define cohesion/alignment/separation, formation/goal, density regulation, and wind compensation. Control follows negative gradients on the manifold with hard constraints.

Safety Layer

  • Control Barrier Functions enforce min‑separation, altitude bands, geofences.
  • Real‑Time Assurance overrides learned/heuristic actions to safe modes (hover/rise/land).
  • Resilient consensus filters outliers/Byzantine nodes to prevent cascades.
  • Fault isolation repairs local graph “holes”; role reassignment maintains coverage/connectivity.

Simulation & Training

Using NVIDIA Omniverse/Isaac: physics (wind, GNSS bias, latency), failure injection (motor/GPS/partitions/spikes), multi‑agent RL + imitation atop the safety layer, and curricula that scale 100→1k→10k agents under tighter latency budgets.

Evidence & Evaluation

  • Safety: violations per flight‑hour, min‑sep breaches, geofence intrusions, k‑failure recovery.
  • Connectivity/resilience: largest connected subgraph over time; completion under partitions.
  • Efficiency: energy, smoothness, throughput. All signed in evidence bundles.

Edge Packaging & Deployment

Export compact policies and safety controllers (INT8/Q4/FP16) with device profiles. Offline policy packs carry default CBFs, geofences, RTA thresholds, logging toggles, and checksums/SBOM. On‑device watchdog controls fail‑safe modes.

Governance & Risk

Geofencing, altitude limits, restricted zones, offline logs with signed digests, SBOM and signatures, license controls. Mitigations include topological limits, sparse comms, robust estimators, authenticated comms, and jurisdictional profiles.
