Insurance Fraud Playbooks: Synthetic Scenarios for Safer Evaluation

By Gwylym Owen — 20–28 min read

Executive Summary

AethergenPlatform provides synthetic playbooks—parameterised fraud scenarios that let investigators and data teams evaluate safely without PHI/PII. Each playbook ships with evidence at operating points and privacy probes so procurement can sign quickly.

Typology Library

  • Upcoding (banded by specialty; overbilling factor).
  • Unbundling (components split; bundle pressure).
  • Phantom providers (distance/time collisions).
  • Doctor shopping (overlapping scripts; device reuse).
  • Duplicate billing (delay windows; modifiers).
  • Kickback rings (referral cycles; abnormal amounts).

Parameters

  • Prevalence and severity distributions.
  • Co‑occurrence rates and timing windows.
  • Regional and plan overlays.

Evidence at OP

  • Detection at fixed FPR budgets (alerts/day).
  • Segment stability across regions and specialties.
  • Cost curves: cases per analyst‑hour.

Privacy

  • Probes (membership/attribute) with CIs.
  • Optional DP budgets per policy.

Playbook YAML (Sketch)

playbooks:
  upcoding:
    prevalence: 0.04
    factor: {min: 1.1, max: 1.5}
  doctor_shopping:
    window_days: 14
    device_reuse: 0.25

Case Study

With playbooks, a payer improved yield +23% at 1% FPR versus legacy rules, while privacy probes stayed under thresholds. Procurement accepted with quarterly refresh and rollback SOPs.

FAQ

Can we tune prevalence on the fly?

Yes—parameters are surfaced in notebooks and dashboards for safe rehearsal.

How do we avoid overfitting to synthetic quirks?

We use ablations and sanity checks; playbooks include limits in evidence.
