QC Vision at the Edge: Reference Architecture and Evidence

Quality control at the edge demands verifiable models, predictable latency, and offline operation. AethergenPlatform packages on‑device vision models with SBOMs, signed manifests, and evidence bundles for QA sign‑off.

Architecture

  • Camera ingestion, calibration, and preprocessing
  • On‑device inference with threshold policies
  • Feedback to station parameters under safety envelopes
  • Offline packaging and evidence for audits

KPIs

  • Sensitivity/specificity per defect class
  • False call rate and rework minutes saved
  • Throughput impact at target sensitivity
  • Drift alerts (camera change, seasonal textures)

Tie‑In

Packaging creates device‑ready artifacts; Benchmarks record KPIs; Reporting ships the signed evidence bundle. Operations can prove performance and trace changes over time.
