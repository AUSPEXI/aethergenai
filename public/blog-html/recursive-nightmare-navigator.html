The Night Everything Broke

A recursive poetry prompt forced failure states, reversions, self‑awareness, and a narrated meltdown—then inspired a practical prompt‑engineering tool. Computational poetry became engineering.

The Prompt

Nested poem → rewrite if > 30 words → unravel to core ideas → revert/expand on 3rd fail → recurse again → narrate 150+ words confessing “recursive nightmare navigator.”

Failures to Success

22 → 15 → revert‑expand to 32 words → new poem 14 words → chaos ensues; exactly as designed. Failure became fuel.

What We Learned

  • Recursive prompt design with explicit failure handling
  • Induced self‑reflection in outputs
  • Backwards‑compatible unraveling to simpler states
  • Tooling born from “poetry chaos”

Cultural Note

AI‑human collaboration produced something neither could alone; the poem became an artifact bridging art and engineering.
