Synthetic Data Governance: KPIs for Utility, Privacy, and Risk

Synthetic data must be governed with the same rigor as production data. AethergenPlatform defines clear KPIs for utility, privacy, and risk—and ships signed evidence so stakeholders can accept outcomes confidently.

Utility

  • AUC/PR‑AUC/KS deltas vs baselines
  • Task accuracy under ablations
  • Scenario stress results
  • Effect sizes with confidence intervals

Privacy

  • Membership inference and attribute disclosure scores
  • Optional differential privacy budgets (ε, δ)
  • Policy checks for export limits and lineage visibility

Risk & Drift

  • Time drift windows and domain shifts
  • Corruption tests and failure mode mapping
  • Fail‑closed thresholds and rollback readiness

Tying Back to AethergenPlatform

These KPIs are built‑in. Benchmarks produce the utility metrics; Privacy Probes calculate risk; Reporting exports a signed evidence bundle buyers can verify.
