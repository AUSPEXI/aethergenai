The Synthetic Data Lifecycle: From Seeds to Evidence

A practical tour of how synthetic data flows through design, generation, validation, and evidence bundling—without exposing PHI/PII.

Lifecycle Stages

  1. Design: multi‑schema with constraints and privacy levels
  2. Seed: minimal, scrubbed samples (or none)
  3. Generate: high‑scale rows with ε control and drift detection
  4. Validate: privacy/utility probes; task eval
  5. Evidence: bundle metrics, lineage, ablations; sign & ship
