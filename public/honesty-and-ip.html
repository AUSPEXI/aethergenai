Honesty & IP‑Safety

This page explains how we talk about capabilities and how we protect intellectual property in public materials.

Honesty

  • No exclusivity claims (for example, “the only platform”) unless independently verified.
  • No “zero‑risk,” “guarantee,” or legal compliance claims. We use “lower‑risk,” “pilot,” and “not legal advice.”
  • We show evidence with reproducible metrics and signed reports where applicable.
  • Research language stays high‑level; we link to public demos and blogs for details.

IP‑Safety

  • No algorithms, formulas, parameters, or circuit paths are published.
  • High‑level invention names may appear; implementation details are intentionally omitted.
  • Press and blogs focus on outcomes, not proprietary methods.
